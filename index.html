Feb. 12, 2022 - Feb 13, 2022

Why attend this seminar?

How specific should assessments and training be to transfer to sport? Which measures of fitness, coordination, and variability are you using to inform training? How are you integrating motor learning principles in your assessments and program design?

Answers to these questions, and much more, will be expanded upon across two days of information-packed, live webinars.

Main Topics

Dynamical Systems Theory

Learn the basics about Dynamical Systems Theory (DST), the Constraints-Led Approach (CLA), and Differential Learning.

General Movement Assessment Overview

What are some pros and cons of existing movement assessments?

Assessing and Programming Movement Variability

How do we assess and interpret movement variability? How can we then enhance movement variability during training?

Designing your own Assessments and Programs

Learn how to integrate and apply theoretical and empirical information in practice.
